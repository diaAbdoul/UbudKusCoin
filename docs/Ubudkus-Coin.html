WHAT IS UBUDKUSCOIN?

Ubudkuscoin is a peer-to-peer Simple Internet currency that uses blockchain technology. Ubudkuscoin is open source, decentralized with no central authority. The math secures networks and empowers individuals to take control of their own finances. Ubudkuscoin features faster transaction confirmation times and improved storage efficiency.

Blockchain

The Ubudkuscoin blockchain is capable of handling high transaction volumes. Due to more frequent block creation, the network supports more transactions.

Open Source Software

Ubudkuscoin is an open source software project released under the MIT license which gives you the ability to run, modify and copy software and distribute, at your choice, modified copies of the software.

Wallet

Wallet encryption allows you to secure your wallet so you can view your transactions and account balance, but it is required to enter your password before spending Ubudkuscoin.

Rewards

Ubudkuscoin uses Proof of Stake or PoS consensus. Does not require a capable computer to create blocks. The selected block maker will get rewards
